Create On-Demand Digital Campaigns With Visual Impact

Reaching your customers with unique, customized creative email campaigns is one of the most effective ways to bring in both new and recurring business. Designed to empower your dealership with user-friendly tools, energybooster delivers sleek, effective campaigns. It seamlessly integrates with a dealer’s existing database, it allows for real-time email delivery to selected groups of vehicle owners. The user can build a customized email promotion or campaign in as little as two to three minutes by first selecting recipients from a dealer’s customer list. A menu-based interfrace then guides the user through the selection of templates, graphics, and suggested text. End result—effortless marketing that works.
