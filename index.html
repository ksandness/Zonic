Don't Let The Good Opportunities Go Unnoticed

By identifying customers with open recalls withing your dealership DMS, recallangel helps you increase customer retention, prioritize eligible recall services, and optimize opportunities for ongoing customer service and growth. With an accurate and current data stream focused on affected recall vehicles and proactive recall communications between your dealership and all impacted customers, recallangel simplifies the process. Additional features include recall-to-repair conversion monitoring and ROI, built-in VIN decoder for effective qualification within VIN range, built-in customer documentation area and tracking, customer contact center performance monitoring, and a user-friendly, cloud-based interface for improved communication.
